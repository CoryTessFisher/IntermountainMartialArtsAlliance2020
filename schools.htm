Schools

Schools associated with the Intermountain Martial Arts Alliance.
 

Cory's Karate

Cory's Karate is a school where we teach the style of Tan Ren Do Karate. Classes are held in Springville, Utah on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday. Please email Cory Fisher at with any specific questions and to check on class availability. Classes are based upon both the age and rank of the students. We have classes appropriate for students age 5 through adult. We have classes for all belt ranks and encourage students to continue to train with us through advanced black belt degrees.
 
 

Kaysville Martial Arts Academy

Kaysville Martial Arts Academy We offer classes for youth ages 5 to 12 years old. We also offer separate classes from youth ages 13 on to adults. Classes are held in the evenings and tuition covers 2 classes per week. Students interested in learning more about the school and system are invited to attend a free introductory class. Call (801) 543-2297 to set up a time.
 
 

Fighting Brothers Karate

Build character and leadership qualities. Martial arts classes benefit growing children far beyond the dojo and in many real-world scenarios. Our structured classes are meant to help develop coordination, physical fitness, mental strength, as well as gain valuable social skills. Through positive reinforcement, we can bring out the best in your children to help them succeed in life. Don't think because you are an adult that you can't join in the fun, we offer classes for all ages and abilities! Plus-Parents of our students are always 50% off!! Martial Arts truly is the sport for anyone. Call (801) 725-1170 for more information.
