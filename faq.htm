What is the Intermountain Martial Arts Alliance?
We are an alliance of martial arts schools who are dedicated to continued improvement. We support each other and help plan events and activities to improve our schools and students.

Who are the member schools of the International Martial Arts Alliance?
Our alliance includes the schools:
  1. Cory's Karate - Springville, Utah
  2. Kaysville Martial Arts Academy - Kaysville, Utah
  3. Fighting Brothers Karate - Kaysville, Utah
  4. Cache Karate - Richmond, Utah

How do I contact members of the Intermountain Martial Arts Alliance?
Use our Contact Us form to contact members of the Intermountain Martial Arts Alliance. Alternatively, contact individual schools through their specific websites.
