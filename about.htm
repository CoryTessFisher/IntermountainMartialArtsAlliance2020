About Us

An alliance of martial artists united in continued improvement.
 

The Intermountain Martial Arts Alliance was formally organized in 2015. We are an organization of martial artists and martial arts schools who are working together to improve ourselves, our students, our schools, and the martial arts community.

We have chosen to embrace the concept of Kaizen. Kaizen is the principle of continued improvement. One never settles for the way things are, even if the current standards are high. There is a commitment to continually raise the standard. One never settles for where they are at. We are "Danketsu shita kaizen" - roughly translated this means "United in Continued Improvement". We all have a genuine love for the martial arts and the effect that martial arts have on those who study them. We each have a desire to unite for continual improvement.

As part of this effort, we meet and discuss ways in which to improve our martial arts schools. We hold multi-school events. We help each other with training, exams, and tournaments. We have developed and defined consistent strategies and requirements for high level students. We have universally committed to a pursuit of excellence in the martial arts.
